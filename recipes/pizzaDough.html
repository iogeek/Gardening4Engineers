Pizza Dough

Ingredients

  • 1 cup room temp water
  • 1/4 c olive oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 1/2 tsp active yeast
  • 3 c flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Mix yeast, water, and sugar in large bowl.
  2. Let yeast become foamy. Usally about 5 minutes.
  3. Add rest of ingredients and mix. Dough should be spft and slighty sticky.
  4. Place in oiled bowl and cover.
  5. Let rise for 1 hour.
